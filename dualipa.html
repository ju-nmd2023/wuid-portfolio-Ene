My Work

This album cover was one of my first ventures into Graphic Design. When Dua Lipa had released her Deluxe edition for the Future Nostalgia album, I was unhappy with how it had turned out, thinking that it did not reflect the vibe of the album. So, I made my own to upload to my music streaming app. I felt as though the iridescent skirt mimmiced a disco ball, mirroring the album's genre
