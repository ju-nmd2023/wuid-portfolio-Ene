About Me

My Name is Ene, I'm 18 years old studying Graphic Design and Web Development at Jonköping University. I come from Kosovo, but I've lived in Hungary and Croatia as well seeing as my mom is the first female ambassador of Kosovo. Im an Aries, I can play the piano and guitar, and I finished the IB. The IB was one of the hardest things I've done, but it taught me how to write reports in a day since I procrastinated a lot.

I've always liked visual art. Art was my favorite class in 9th grade, and also the one I excelled in. Other than the arts, I liveeee for psychology. It was my favorite high school subject, and also the one I scored highest on. Who knew it would come in handy in this programme. When Bruce talked about dual-processing theory, who yassed? Me, I yassed. Giving and serving is what I do. In fact, some of my serves are right below this paragraph.

My Favorite Media

One thing about me is that I love consuming media. Yum yum, delish dopamine for my receptors. My favorite singers at the moment would have to be Marina Diamandis, Monika Liu, Metronomy, and lowkey am having a Beyoncé resurgence at the moment. There really is a reason she's so respected and stanned. For more insights into my music taste, you can always click my spotify link in the header.

When it comes to movies and TV shows, that's pretty simple. My favorite movie of all time has to be Ocean's 8. I can quote it start-to-finish and watch it over and over without ever getting bored. In this vein, I have to mention The Devil Wears Prada as well, classic comfort movie I live for. Finally, Coraline must have her moment as well. I would say underrated but I really do think its appropriately rated, everyone loves that movie. As far as TV shows go, I have 3 main ones that I adore; Russian Doll, The Good Place, and Pushing Daisies. All gorg, all flawless.
